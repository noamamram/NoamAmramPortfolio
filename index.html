My Games

Game 1: Where's Bathrooma?

Welcome to the quirky and adventurous world of 'Where's Bathrooma?'! Step into the shoes of our protagonist, Bathrooman, a clever play on words for the man from the bathroom sign. In this captivating game, players embark on a thrilling journey through a series of challenging levels, each featuring a 3D, life-sized bathroom meticulously crafted in Blender. The objective is to locate Bathrooma, a clever twist on the woman from the bathroom sign, who somehow finds herself lost in this puzzling bathroom. As the player explores the intricately designed bathroom, they must use their wits to solve puzzles, decipher clues, and unravel hidden secrets, much like navigating an escape room. 'Where's Bathrooma?' promises an exciting and humor-filled experience, encouraging players to put their detective skills to the test and reunite Bathrooman with his counterpart, Bathrooma, in an unforgettable virtual escapade.

Game 2: VRange

Welcome to the adrenaline-pumping virtual world of "VRange," a clever play on words for a VR shoot range simulator! Immerse yourself in the ultimate shooting experience, as this game takes realistic shooting to a whole new level. Step into the virtual range room, thoughtfully designed using Blender software, and get ready for the action. The game offers a wide array of authentic mechanics, requiring players to perform lifelike actions to fire their weapons. You'll have to move the weapon to a single position, insert cartridges, and even step on the weapon to brace for a steady shot, just like in reality. Your mission is to hit the targets as accurately and swiftly as possible within the intense 2-minute time limit. Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score and sharpen your shooting skills. If you seek more casual fun, "VRange" offers a "free play" mode, where you can practice shooting at a variety of entertaining objects. Get your VR headset on and prepare for an exhilarating shooting adventure like never before!
